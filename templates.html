Quick Reference

Outreach Do's and Don'ts

Do

  • Research the brand contact's name (LinkedIn, company website)
  • Reference specific products you genuinely use/like
  • Include hard numbers (viewers, demographics, engagement rates)
  • Follow up exactly twice, then move on
  • Negotiate — first offer is rarely final
  • Send post-campaign reports (this alone sets you apart from 90% of creators)

Don't

  • Send generic mass emails (personalize every one)
  • Undersell yourself (know your CPM benchmarks)
  • Accept product-only deals once you have 1K+ avg viewers
  • Agree to exclusivity without premium pricing
  • Skip FTC disclosures (non-negotiable, legally required)
  • Burn bridges — a "no" today is a "yes" at 10x your audience size
