  • You get $0.01/Bit (100%)

  • ~$3.50 CPM (gross)
  • ~$1.75/1K views (standard 50/50)

  • Q4 highest CPMs, Q1 lowest

Third-Party Donations

  • StreamElements / Streamlabs
  • You keep ~97% (processor fees only)
  • Twitch takes $0
  • Ko-fi: 100% (free tier)

Sponsorships

  • Micro (10-100 CCV): $50-$250/stream
  • Small (100-500): $250-$1K/stream
  • Mid (500-2K): $1K-$5K/stream
  • Large (2K-10K): $5K-$25K/stream
  • Top (10K+): $25K-$100K+/campaign

Other Streams

  • Merch (+32% with alerts enabled)
  • Affiliate marketing (5-15% commission)
  • Patreon / Memberships
  • Coaching ($20-$100+/hr)
  • Events & Appearances

Section 10

Content Restrictions & Limitations

What you can and cannot do — critical compliance knowledge

Banned Activities

  • Unlicensed casino streaming (Stake, Rollbit, etc.)
  • Nudity, pornography, sexual services
  • Consuming / selling illegal drugs
  • MLM / pyramid scheme promotion
  • Selling account access / boosting
  • Hate speech / harassment (zero tolerance)

Allowed Commercial Activities

  • Merch via merch shelf — allowed
  • External store links — allowed
  • Sponsored content — must use Branded Content tool
  • Affiliate links — must disclose per FTC
  • Direct sales pitches — cannot be primary purpose
⚖️
FTC Disclosure: Non-Negotiable Persistent on-screen disclosure ("#ad" or "#sponsored" overlay) required during ALL sponsored content. "Thanks to [brand]" at the start of the stream alone is NOT sufficient. Fines up to $50,000+ per violation.

Simulcasting Rules (Updated 2025)

Allowed

  • Simulcast to YouTube, Kick, etc.
  • Both Affiliates and Partners
  • Link to other channels in About section
  • Maintain Twitch quality equal or better

Prohibited

  • Merging chats from multiple platforms
  • Directing viewers to leave Twitch
  • Lower quality on Twitch than elsewhere
  • Violating signed exclusivity agreements
Section 11

Growth Tactics That Actually Work

And the mistakes that will kill your channel

Growth Tactics by Stage

Tier 1: Foundation

  • Consistent schedule (same time, every time)
  • Interact with EVERY chatter
  • Raid 2-3 streamers after each stream
  • Post clips on X/YouTube/TikTok daily
  • Stream in right category (top 10-20)
  • Engage in other communities genuinely

Tier 2: Acceleration (100+ CCV)

  • Collab with 2-5x sized streamers
  • Weekly YouTube long-form content
  • Build daily-active Discord
  • Run community events weekly
  • A/B test titles and thumbnails

Tier 3: Scale (500+ CCV)

  • Cross-platform content engine
  • Active brand deal pursuit
  • Launch merchandise
  • Attend conventions
  • Build a team (3-5 people)

What Works

  • Consistent schedule (68% of subs watch 80%+ of streams)
  • Authentic personality (audiences detect fakes fast)
  • Cross-platform clips (3-5 impressions before follow)
  • Niche category selection (be visible, not invisible)
  • Genuine networking (raid, collab, participate)
  • Audio quality priority (viewers leave for bad audio)
  • Community building (Discord, inside jokes, recognition)

What Kills Growth

  • Follow-for-follow (dead followers hurt algorithm)
  • Buying followers/viewers (bannable, destroys ranking)
  • 8+ hours daily from day 1 (unsustainable burnout)
  • Only streaming, no cross-platform content
  • Switching games/content constantly
  • Begging for follows/subs (repels high-value viewers)
  • Giveaway-only growth (attracts freebie hunters)
